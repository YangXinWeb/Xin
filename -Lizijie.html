Lizijie Project

Date: 2021-

Medium: forest, stele, magnets, human, time, etc

Dimension: varies

Lizijie - Emergence Project is a long-term experiment that wants to discuss the interaction between nature and humans' activities. Magnetic fields, as adventitious interferons, will be added to one region of a forest. Like many things humans took from or added to nature, magnetic fields are added in the forest and will be observed. The stele is built as a marker of the land and the time.
