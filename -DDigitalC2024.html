Chapter 3: The Signal and the Silence

  • User: The protagonist
  • The Interface: A cold, a corporate system
  • Echo: A shadowy presence 
            ... 

            User(Monologue):
            Do you remember how many times
            How much effort it took to become modern
            To not be left behind, to keep pace with a world that moves faster than thought
            I remember when I first logged in
            The world at my fingertips, they said
            Freedom, choice, connection
            But it’s not my fingers that move anymore
            It’s theirs
            
            Interface:
            Welcome back, valued participant
            Your contributions have been noted
            Please proceed.
            
            User (Monologue):
            Noted? 
            Contributions
            I only wanted
            What did I want
            
            Echo:
            You wanted to keep up.
            Now you’re running in circles
            You wanted to belong
            Now you’re a cog
            You wanted to connect
            Now you’re the signal
 
            User (Monologue):
            But I gave them everything
            My voice, my laughter
            Even the quiet moments between my thoughts
            Where did it go      
            
            User (Monologue):
            They said I agreed
            They said I clicked
            Yes, I clicked
            But did I understand
            
            User (Monologue):
            No
            I didn’t know it meant my dreams would be monetized
            That my fears would be targeted
            That my shadow
            Even my shadow
            Would be sold

            Interface:
            Processing
                     
            Echo:
            But shadows make great currency
            They stretch long
            They leave no trace

            User (Monologue):
            I just wanted to be seen
            Now I am
            Everywhere       

            Interface:
            Visibility achieved
            Privacy irrelevant

            User (Monologue):
            They call it progress
            I call it extraction
            They mine my life
            Not for gold
            But for patterns

            Interface:
            Congratulations
            Your uniqueness has been uploaded
            Your essence has been optimized

            User (Monologue):
            Is that all I am
            An essence
            A data point

            Echo:
            You are the product
            The fuel
            The end

            User (Monologue):
            But I am still here
            Aren’t I

            Interface:
            Error
            User not found

            Echo:
            They’ll find another
